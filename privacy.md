    <h2 class="privacy-title">4. Client-Side Storage (localStorage)</h2>
    <p>
      This website stores a single user interface preference — your selected colour theme (light or dark mode) — in your browser's <strong>localStorage</strong>. This is a client-side storage mechanism; the data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.
    </p>
    <p>
      <strong>Data stored:</strong> One key–value pair: <code>theme: "light"</code> or <code>theme: "dark"</code>.<br>
      <strong>Purpose:</strong> Remembering your display preference between page visits to avoid visual flash and improve user experience.<br>
      <strong>Legal basis:</strong> § 25(2)(2) TTDSG — the storage is strictly necessary to provide the functionality explicitly requested by the user (theme selection). No consent is required.<br>
      <strong>Retention:</strong> Stored indefinitely in your browser until you manually clear your browser's localStorage or change the preference. You can clear it at any time via your browser's developer tools or storage settings.
    </p>
