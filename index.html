Hi, I'm a team and project manager from Berlin!

I have expertise in different fields of renewable energy and mechanical engineering. The projects I worked with included heatpumps, CHP, storage solutions, cooling systems, solar thermal, wave and wind energy systems.

Renewable Energies are necessary to ensure a sustainable future. As an engineer I believe in innovative energy management and concepts to integrate energy efficient technology in our daily life.

I'm a well organised and efficient project manager. I believe that an open communication with all stakeholders is the key to project success.

As a team leader it is important to me that my team has all the tools and knwolegde they need in order to be successful. Giving each other support, being open-minded and respectful while not losing your humor are important values to a great team.

I'm an open-minded person who likes to study and implement new technologies and approaches. The balance between scientific research and applied technology is important to me.

I support equal opportunities for all genders. As a member of the "Frauennetzwerk Energie", "hypatia - Frauennetzwerk Erneuerbare Energien und Cleantech e.V." and "Women of New Energies e.V." I am part of a growing network of amazing women who each other in the professionally.
